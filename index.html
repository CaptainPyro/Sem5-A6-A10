Good Hunter

Mondstadt



Good Hunter is a shop that can be found in the City of Mondstadt. They sell simple Food, Cooking Ingredients and Cooking recipes. Sara is a waitress here.
Other than the usual adventurer's favourites such as the Fisherman's Toast and the Chicken-Mushroom Skewer, they also server delicacies such as the Flaming Red Bolognese and Sautéed Matsutake which are unique to Mondstadt itself. Not only they serve dishes but are also kind to share a written note of how to make the same.

About Head Waitress Sara

Sara is a friendly, light-hearted woman. She is confident about Good Hunter's reputation and dishes. As the waitress of a very popular restaurant, she is well informed of what happens in Mondstadt without being there, such as Jean's respect for the Traveler, and hearing about Charles' worries.
